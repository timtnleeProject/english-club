Nov. 24, 2008

Surprise, Surprise

After my little white bird died of serious illness, I bought another male bird to keep company with the lonely one that was left behind, whose name, Hsiao Ding Ding, means hsiao zhuang ding 小壯丁, or able-bodied boy.

Usually it took a long period of time for a new bird and an old one to adjust to each other and even play together. Unexpectedly, they got along well quickly. While they were in the cage, they always sat or stood together closely and even combed each other’s feathers. At times, one of them would close its eyes to enjoy the other’s softly pecking its feathers. What surprised me especially was that although I prepared them two bowls to take a bath every day, they preferred to take their bath in one bowl instead. Observing the extreme intimacy between them, we decided the new bird must be female and named her accordingly Hsiao Ya Tou.

They lived together happily, but Hsio Ya Tou got sick suddenly. She had diarrhea and slept all the time. Because of a lot of painful experiences from the past, I dare not take her to see the stupid vet who had given my two birds a fatal treatment. Following the advice of the bird shopkeeper, I put the cage under the soft light to keep my sick bird warm and fed her the most nutritious food. Thank God. She got well gradually.

I had no doubt that they would live happily ever after. To my astonishment, however, one day I heard a noise of flying and jumping around in the birdcage. Even though it was not a hard pecking, my Hsiao Ting Ting was so scared that he flew up and down constantly to escape from Hsiao Ya Tou’s pecking. I never really figured out what resulted in Hsiao Ting Ting’s fright that drove him to attempt to escape from the cage. Maybe Hsiao Ya Tou was in fact a male bird and was much stronger than Hsiao Ting Ting.

To keep my Hsiao Ting Ting from getting hurt, I had no choice but to place them in two cages. Seeing that they were back flying and playing together peacefully outside the cage, I tried many times to put them back into one cage in hope of making them live together again but it did not work. It was so funny that the seemingly so sweet couple now lives in separate cages. Maybe one day they will want to live in the same cage again.
