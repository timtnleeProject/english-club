Oct. 1, 2009

Tickets

For my daughter’s wedding, my son and his family came back home from Canada. After the wedding, my daughter would go to Paris for honeymoon while my son, together with his family, would go back to Vancouver for work. Before the actual wedding, we all were busy with preparations and lots of chores. I only knew they would be leaving on the 23rd of September separately.

Before setting off, my son had lived with his parents-in-law in Tainan for two days. So he got to the airport with his relatives in Tainan. It was to kill two birds with one stone that we decided to see off both the newly-wed couple and my son’s family at the airport.

While we all got together to have some dessert, we found that they all took the Eva Air at 23:55 p.m. to Paris and Vancouver respectively. They would go into the customs at the same time and had the same boarding time, 23:25 p.m. As a matter of fact, they didn’t arrange it to happen when they bought the tickets one month ago. To my surprise again, they even carried luggage of the same type and same color which they got from the Taishin Bank while applying for the Taishin card.That was really an amazing day. We took a lot of pictures of their air tickets. We all thought if we bought the lottery with the numbers 49 23 25 55 10 87 (all numbers shown on the tickets), we might have a great chance of winning.
