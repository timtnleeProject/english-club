My Hobbies

I have several hobbies that make my life happy and healthy. The first one is Chinese martial arts. I have practiced it for about eight years. Every Sunday afternoon, I go to a Tai Chi club in my church. There we played Tai Chi along with beautiful music. Though being sweaty and exhausted, everyone feels great each time.

My second hobby is keeping pets. I have two dogs, one parrot, and a school of fish. By taking care of them, I get a chance to love and play. I play tug-of-war with my dogs with a rag. I often challenge my parrot with skillful whistling. Every time I watch my fish swimming, I feel peaceful.

The third one: learning English. I have been listening to English channels for several years. I have absorbed a lot of new knowledge by learning English too. I feel these are indeed great hobbies to keep. They have made my life wonderful.
